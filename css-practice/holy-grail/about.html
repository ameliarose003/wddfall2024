About Me!

Meet Miranda, the heart and hands behind The Cottage Bakery! At just 16 years old, Miranda brings her passion for baking to life in her small-town kitchen in Cameron. Inspired by her love for creating homemade treats and sharing them with her community, she has turned her dream into a delightful reality. Every pastry, cake, and loaf of bread reflects her dedication to quality and the warmth of home. Miranda is excited to bring a little sweetness to your day, one delicious bite at a time!
