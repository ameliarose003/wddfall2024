The Cottage Bakery Site Plan

By Ami Halsey

WDD 131-2pm class

Overview

Purpose

My website's purpose is to help a new bakery business get started and have a place to better reach others and make orders.

Audience

My audience is anyone who loves baked goods.

Dynamic elements

The Javascript will be used in several places including, but not limited to a search engine that sorts and filters the available baked goods. It will also pull from a .mjs where the lists of The Cottage Bakery's baked goods will be stored.

Content

Home page

Welcome to The Cottage Bakery, where every treat is crafted with love and a touch of home. Nestled in the heart of our community, we take pride in baking fresh, delicious goods daily. From warm, flaky pastries to decadent cakes and artisan bread, our creations are made with the finest ingredients to bring joy to your table. Whether you're here to indulge, celebrate, or simply enjoy the comforting aroma of baked perfection, Cottage Bakery is delighted to share a slice of happiness with you.

About

Meet Miranda, the heart and hands behind The Cottage Bakery! At just 16 years old, Miranda brings her passion for baking to life in her small-town kitchen in Cameron. Inspired by her love for creating homemade treats and sharing them with her community, she has turned her dream into a delightful reality. Every pastry, cake, and loaf of bread reflects her dedication to quality and the warmth of home. Miranda is excited to bring a little sweetness to your day, one delicious bite at a time!

Wireframes

Create two wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

The home page will include a "welcome to the site" paragraph and a search bar to search the different goodies sold. On the side there will be image links to specials on the Menu page. I plan on adding a big image to the start that has the welcome sign on it to make it more interesting and then they can scroll down to see the rest of the page with the search bar for the treats that are wanted.

About

The about page will include a picture of the owner of the bakery and her story of how it started, as well as contact info etc. There will also be the large image on this page, I'm still deciding to add it on both or just this one.

Menu

The Menu page will include the deals and specials in general and weekly with attatched images that make them look delicious.

The menu page will include the images and descriptions of the different treats that the bakery offers.
